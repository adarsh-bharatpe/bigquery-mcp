What competitors do — UPI retention & engagement hooks

Paytm

  • Gold coins as cashback on payments & tasks
  • Scratch cards, lucky draws post-txn
  • Paytm Postpaid / wallet float for repeat use
  • Merchant QR cashback & festival campaigns

PhonePe

  • PhonePe Rewards / gift boxes after payments
  • Switch integration (apps, recharges, insurance)
  • Referral bonuses for first payment
  • UPI lite + autopay for low-friction repeat

CRED

  • ₹50 cashback on first 5 UPI payments (new users)
  • CRED coins → burn on catalogue / bill pay
  • Credit-card-first positioning; streaks & milestones

Navi

  • Navi coins convertible to cash / discounts
  • UPI + lending cross-sell; bill pay nudges

Google Pay

  • ₹1 nudge to pay a contact / loved one
  • Scan QR, pay contact, mobile recharge prompts
  • Scratch cards, festival campaigns, “#Tez” style challenges
  • Deep linking from Search / Notifications

SuperMoney

  • Actual cashback credited to bank account
  • Transparent ₹-back vs coin abstraction

Amazon Pay / Flipkart UPI

  • E-commerce wallet cashback on UPI checkout
  • Loyalty points stack with platform orders

BHIM / bank apps

  • Rupay-linked offers, LPG subsidy rails
  • Government campaign cashback windows

Pattern: immediate ₹ reward, 2nd txn in 7d, utility anchor, multi-rail habit.

Key takeaway: Competitors win with ₹ cashback, 2nd-txn nudges, coins, and utility anchors — mirror on Power / Mixture / Zillion cohorts.

What needs to be done?

🎯

Fix the funnel (week 0–1)

  • First outward SUCCESS — reduce FAILED-first (~22%); better error UX
  • Target 2+ payout SUCCESS in 30d (escape Light ~2.6% M+1)
  • Multi-rail discovery: QR + UPI ID + recharge (not CONTACT-only ~2.3%)
🎁

Rewards & Zillion

  • Expand redemption in first 30d (~3% today, ~78% M+1 when redeemed)
  • Test ₹ cashback on 2nd & 5th txn (CRED / SuperMoney pattern)
⚡

Cross-sell & utility

  • Push BBPS after first UPI success (~17% W+1 — grow attach)
  • Telecom / grocery MCC journeys (~35% M+1) as template campaigns
💎

High-LTV cohorts

  • Retain Power / Mixture / multi-account with priority support
  • Credit-dominant linkers (~45% M+1) — tailored credit-line UX
  • Measure payout-only Zillion earn; fix pay-in leakage
Key takeaway: North star — lift payout M+1 (~28–33%) toward VPA M1 (~35–42%) via first-pay success, 2+ txns in 30d, Zillion redeem, and BBPS attach.
